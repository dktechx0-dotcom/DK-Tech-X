FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our SEO services.

How long does it take to see SEO results?

SEO is a long-term strategy. You can typically expect to see initial improvements in rankings within 2-4 months, with significant results visible after 6-9 months. This timeline varies based on competition, industry, website age, and current SEO status. We provide monthly progress reports so you can track every improvement.

Do you guarantee #1 rankings on Google?

No ethical SEO agency can guarantee #1 rankings, as search algorithms are controlled by Google. However, we have a proven track record of achieving top rankings for our clients using white-hat SEO techniques. We guarantee transparent reporting, continuous optimization, and measurable improvements in traffic and rankings.

What is included in your SEO audit?

Our comprehensive SEO audit covers: technical SEO analysis (crawl errors, site speed, mobile-friendliness, Core Web Vitals), on-page SEO evaluation (meta tags, content quality, keyword usage, internal linking), off-page SEO assessment (backlink profile, domain authority), and competitor analysis. You receive a detailed report with prioritized recommendations.

Do you use white-hat SEO techniques?

Absolutely! We strictly follow Google's Webmaster Guidelines and use only ethical, white-hat SEO techniques. We never use black-hat tactics like keyword stuffing, cloaking, PBNs, or buying links. Our strategies are designed for sustainable, long-term growth without risking Google penalties.

Can SEO work for my specific industry?

Yes! SEO works for virtually every industry—e-commerce, healthcare, real estate, education, finance, SaaS, legal, hospitality, and more. We customize our SEO strategies based on your industry's unique keywords, audience behavior, competition level, and search intent. Every strategy is tailored to your specific market.

Do you provide monthly reports?

Yes! Every client receives a detailed monthly SEO report that includes: keyword ranking changes, organic traffic trends, backlinks acquired, technical improvements made, content published, and actionable recommendations for the next month. We also offer monthly strategy calls to discuss progress and next steps.
