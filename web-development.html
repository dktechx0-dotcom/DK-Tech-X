FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our web development services.

How long does it take to build a website?

A simple 3-5 page website typically takes 1-2 weeks. A professional business website (10+ pages) takes 3-4 weeks. Complex web applications or e-commerce platforms may take 6-8 weeks depending on requirements. We always provide a detailed timeline during the proposal phase.

Do you provide website hosting and domain?

While we don't sell hosting directly, we help you set up the best hosting for your needs (we recommend providers like Hostinger, SiteGround, or AWS). We also assist with domain registration and configuration. Our team ensures everything is properly connected and optimized.

Will my website be mobile-friendly and SEO-optimized?

Absolutely! Every website we build is fully responsive and tested across all devices (mobile, tablet, desktop). We follow SEO best practices including proper heading structure, meta tags, schema markup, image optimization, fast loading speeds, and clean code that search engines love.

Can I update the website content myself after launch?

Yes! If you choose a CMS-based solution (WordPress, etc.), you'll get a user-friendly admin panel to edit text, images, blog posts, and more without any coding knowledge. We provide video tutorials and documentation for your team. For custom solutions, we can build a custom admin panel.

What if I need changes after the website is live?

All our packages include a free support period (1-6 months depending on the plan). During this time, we handle minor changes at no extra cost. After the support period, we offer affordable maintenance packages or hourly rates for ongoing updates and enhancements.

Do you redesign existing websites?

Yes, we specialize in website redesigns! We can give your outdated website a modern, high-performance makeover while preserving your existing content and SEO rankings. We'll analyze your current site, identify improvements, and build a new version that exceeds expectations.
