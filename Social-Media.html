FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our social media marketing services.

Which social media platforms should my business be on?

It depends on your target audience and business goals. We recommend: Facebook for broad audience reach, Instagram for visual brands and younger demographics, LinkedIn for B2B and professional services, YouTube for video content, TikTok for Gen Z engagement, and Pinterest for lifestyle/e-commerce brands. We'll analyze your audience and recommend the best platforms during our strategy phase.

How often should I post on social media?

Posting frequency varies by platform. We typically recommend: Instagram—5-7 posts + daily stories per week, Facebook—3-5 posts per week, LinkedIn—3-4 posts per week, Twitter/X—1-3 posts per day, TikTok—3-5 posts per week. Quality always trumps quantity. We create a customized posting schedule based on your audience's behavior and platform algorithms.

Do you provide content creation services?

Absolutely! Our team includes graphic designers, video editors, copywriters, and content strategists who create all your social media content. We handle everything from custom graphics, professional photography, short-form videos, reels, stories, carousels, and compelling captions. You get a complete content package tailored to your brand.

How do you measure social media success?

We track key metrics aligned with your goals: engagement rate (likes, comments, shares, saves), follower growth, reach & impressions, click-through rate, conversion rate, website traffic from social, and ROI for paid campaigns. You receive a comprehensive monthly report with insights, trends, and actionable recommendations.

Can you manage our existing social media accounts?

Yes! We can take over management of your existing social media accounts seamlessly. We'll conduct an audit, understand your current performance, and develop a strategy to improve and grow your presence. We work as an extension of your team, maintaining your brand voice while bringing fresh ideas and strategies.

How quickly can I see results from social media marketing?

Social media is a long-term investment. You can expect initial engagement improvements within 1-3 months, significant follower growth within 6-9 months, and strong brand authority within 12 months. Paid social campaigns can deliver immediate results in terms of reach and conversions. We provide monthly progress reports so you can track growth consistently.
