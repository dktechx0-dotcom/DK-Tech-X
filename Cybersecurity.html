Frequently Asked Questions

What is penetration testing?
Penetration testing is a simulated cyber attack against your systems to identify vulnerabilities that could be exploited by real attackers.
How often should security audits be done?
We recommend quarterly security audits for most businesses, with more frequent testing for high-risk industries like finance and healthcare.
What is included in 24/7 monitoring?
Our SOC team monitors your network, endpoints, and cloud infrastructure around the clock for suspicious activities and potential threats.
Do you help with compliance certification?
Yes, we provide gap analysis, remediation guidance, and documentation support for GDPR, HIPAA, PCI-DSS, SOC 2, and ISO 27001.
What happens during a security breach?
Our incident response team immediately isolates affected systems, investigates the breach, contains the threat, and restores operations.
Is employee training really necessary?
Absolutely. Over 90% of data breaches involve human error. Security awareness training significantly reduces this risk.
