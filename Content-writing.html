FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our content writing services.

Do you provide SEO-optimized content?

Absolutely! Every piece of content we create is optimized for search engines. This includes keyword research and integration, proper heading structure (H1, H2, H3), meta descriptions, internal and external linking, image alt text suggestions, and readability optimization. Our content is designed to rank well while engaging readers.

What is your turnaround time for content?

Standard turnaround time is 3-5 business days for blog posts and website copy. Longer content like white papers and e-books may take 7-10 business days. We also offer express delivery (48 hours) for urgent projects at a small premium. We always agree on deadlines upfront and deliver on time.

Is the content 100% plagiarism-free?

Yes, absolutely! Every piece of content we deliver is 100% original and written from scratch. We use professional plagiarism checking tools (Copyscape, Grammarly Premium) to verify originality before delivery. You receive completely unique content that passes all plagiarism checks. We never use AI to generate content without human editing.

Can you match our brand voice and tone?

Yes! Before starting, we study your brand guidelines, existing content, target audience, and preferred tone. Whether you need a professional, conversational, witty, technical, or authoritative voice—our writers adapt to match your brand perfectly. We can also create a brand voice guide if you don't have one yet.

What industries do you write content for?

We have expert writers across diverse industries including technology, healthcare, finance, e-commerce, SaaS, real estate, education, travel, fashion, food, and more. If your industry requires specialized knowledge, we'll assign a writer with relevant experience and subject matter expertise to ensure accuracy and depth.

What if I'm not satisfied with the content?

Your satisfaction is our priority. All our packages include free revisions—2 revisions per piece for Starter/Professional plans and unlimited revisions for Enterprise. If you're still not satisfied after revisions, we offer a rewrite or credit toward future work. We work closely with you until you're 100% happy with the content.
