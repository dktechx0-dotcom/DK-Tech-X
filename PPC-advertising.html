FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our PPC advertising services.

What is the minimum ad budget you recommend?

We recommend a minimum monthly ad spend of ₹10,000-₹25,000 for meaningful PPC results. This allows us to gather sufficient data for optimization and testing. The ideal budget depends on your industry, competition, target keywords, and goals. We'll help you determine the optimal budget during our strategy phase that balances reach with ROI.

How quickly can I see results from PPC advertising?

Unlike SEO, PPC delivers immediate results. Your ads can start appearing within hours of campaign launch and generating clicks and conversions from day one. However, the first 2-4 weeks are typically an optimization phase where we gather data, test different approaches, and refine targeting to improve performance and reduce costs.

Which ad platform is best for my business?

It depends on your business type and goals: Google Ads is excellent for capturing high-intent search traffic (people actively searching for your products/services). Facebook/Instagram Ads are great for brand awareness, visual products, and B2C targeting. LinkedIn Ads work best for B2B lead generation. We analyze your target audience and recommend the optimal platform mix for maximum ROI.

Do you manage our ad spend or do we pay the platforms directly?

Your ad spend goes directly to the advertising platforms (Google, Meta, LinkedIn, etc.) through your own ad accounts. You maintain full control and ownership of your accounts and billing. We simply manage, optimize, and report on the campaigns. Our management fee is separate and covers our expertise, strategy, and optimization work.

How do you measure PPC campaign success?

We track key performance indicators aligned with your goals: Return on Ad Spend (ROAS), Cost Per Click (CPC), Click-Through Rate (CTR), Cost Per Acquisition/Lead (CPA/CPL), Conversion Rate, Quality Score, and Impression Share. You receive detailed monthly reports with insights, trends, and recommendations for continuous improvement.

Can you work with our existing Google Ads account?

Absolutely! We can audit and optimize your existing Google Ads, Facebook Ads, or any other PPC account. We'll review your current campaigns, identify wasted spend, improve underperforming ads, and scale what's working. There's no need to start from scratch—we build upon your existing foundation for better results.
