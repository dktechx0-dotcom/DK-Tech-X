Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our services.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A typical web application takes 6-12 weeks, while AI/ML projects may take 3-6 months. We provide a detailed timeline during the discovery phase.

Do you offer custom pricing?

Yes! We understand every business is unique. Contact our sales team for a customized quote tailored to your specific requirements and budget.

What is your development methodology?

We follow Agile methodology with 2-week sprints. You'll receive regular updates, demo sessions, and have full visibility into the development process.

Can you work with our existing systems?

Absolutely! We specialize in integrating new solutions with legacy systems, ensuring smooth data migration and minimal disruption to your operations.

What kind of support do you provide after launch?

We offer comprehensive post-launch support including bug fixes, performance monitoring, security updates, and feature enhancements based on your plan.
