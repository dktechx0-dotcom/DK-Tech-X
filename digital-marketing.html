FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our digital marketing services.

How long does it take to see SEO results?

SEO is a long-term strategy. You can typically expect to see initial improvements in rankings within 3-4 months, with significant results visible after 6-9 months. This varies based on competition, industry, and current website status. We provide monthly progress reports so you can track improvements.

What is the minimum budget for PPC advertising?

We recommend a minimum ad spend of ₹5,000-₹10,000 per month for meaningful PPC results. This allows us to gather sufficient data for optimization. The actual budget depends on your industry, competition, and goals. We'll help you determine the optimal budget during our strategy phase.

Do you offer contract-free monthly plans?

Yes! All our marketing plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. We believe in earning your business every month through results. You can cancel anytime with 30 days' notice. However, we recommend a minimum 3-6 month commitment to see meaningful marketing results.

How do you measure marketing success?

We track key performance indicators (KPIs) aligned with your business goals: website traffic, keyword rankings, conversion rates, cost per lead/acquisition, return on ad spend (ROAS), engagement rates, and revenue attribution. You receive a comprehensive monthly report with insights and recommendations.

Can you work with our existing marketing team?

Absolutely! We frequently collaborate with in-house marketing teams to supplement their efforts. We can handle specific channels (like SEO or PPC) while your team manages others, or we can act as an extension of your team for additional bandwidth and expertise.

What industries do you specialize in?

We've successfully delivered results across diverse industries including e-commerce, SaaS, healthcare, real estate, education, finance, hospitality, and professional services. Our strategies are customized to each industry's unique audience, competition, and regulations.
